Story

We're ExamFX, a company that understands that in the insurance and financial services industries, the success of business is founded on a solid workforce. That's where we come in: We get your people licensed so they go to work faster. Our story began in the insurance business, when we pioneered an industry-changing, state-specific pre-licensing exam - The Exam Simulator. It was the first of its kind to provide aspiring insurance professionals with an authentic and realistic exam experience from test questions to test format. Since then, our educational tools have expanded to include rich multimedia content that appeals to a variety of learning styles as well as other branches of the financial services industry. We've taken the lessons and testing out of the classroom to create a personalized learning experience for your employees. They can study in their living rooms or a break rooms, and at the pace that is right for them. We know the more quickly they learn and pass their license exams, the more successful your business will become. We are a part of Ascend Learning - the nation's premier technology-based learning solutions provider dedicated to higher performance student results.

Leaders In Online Training

Since we're number one, so are you. ExamFX is the preeminent online education and pre-licensing company in the nation. We boast a 96% satisfaction rate. In the insurance industry, ExamFX pre-licensing study materials have helped over 1.5 million people prepare for their state licensing exams, many of whom have passed on their very first try. In the world of financial services, we offer the best in securities exam preparation. We cover all topics listed on the exam content outlines so your employees have the knowledge they need to pass and succeed. Everybody wins.
